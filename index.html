whatAcountry – Privacy Policy

whatAcountry does not collect, share, or sell any personal data.

All game progress and settings are stored locally on your device.

All country data (names, capitals, populations, flags, etc.) is bundled directly within the app and loaded offline — no external data services are contacted for this content.

The app loads map tiles from Apple Maps for display purposes only. No user data is sent to Apple Maps beyond what is necessary to render the map.

The app does not use analytics, advertising SDKs, or tracking of any kind.
