Privacy Policy

whatAcountry does not collect, share, or sell any personal data.

All quiz progress and settings are stored locally on your device.

All country data is bundled directly within the app and loaded offline.

Map tiles are loaded from Apple Maps and OpenStreetMap for display only. No user data is transmitted.

Tip Jar purchases are handled by Apple. The developer has no access to payment information.

The app does not use analytics, advertising SDKs, or tracking of any kind.
