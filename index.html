whatAcountry – Privacy Policy

whatAcountry does not collect, share, or sell any personal data.

All game progress and settings are stored locally on your device.

All country data is bundled directly within the app and loaded offline.

The app loads map tiles from Apple Maps and OpenStreetMap for display purposes only. No user data is sent beyond what is necessary to render the map.

The app does not use analytics, advertising SDKs, or tracking of any kind.
